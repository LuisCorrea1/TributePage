Sergio "Checo" Pérez

Biography

Sergio Pérez Mendoza, nicknamed Checo (born January 26, 1990 in Guadalajara, México), is currently a driver for Oracle Red Bull Racing alongside Max Verstappen. Pérez was one of four drivers who débuted in 2011 for Sauber, with the others being Pastor Maldonado, Paul di Resta and Jérôme d'Ambrosio. He is the first Mexican driver in Formula One in 30 years since Héctor Rebaque in 1981. He scored his first podium at the 2012 Malaysian Grand Prix, being the first Mexican to do so in more than 40 years. He scored his first pole position in the 2022 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix in surprise fashion narrowly beating Charles Leclerc. His first win came at the 2020 Sakhir Grand Prix, where he made one of his best comebacks comming from the bottom of the grid.
